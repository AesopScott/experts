Navigation accent study

Ten gold shimmer accents for the compact grouped nav.

Each option keeps the current Dark Authority nav layout and adds slow animated curved gold bars above and below the buttons. The goal is a refined accent, not a flashy effect.

Same nav, different accent The buttons, labels, dark shell, and four groups stay fixed. Only the gold bars change: thickness, glow, length, curve, and shimmer speed.
