The sponsorship logic

We are not only funding content. We are funding distribution.

The fastest path to global AI literacy is to partner with people who already have trust. 25experts gives AI influencers a better home for their patrons than scattered feeds, YouTube, or Patreon alone. Sponsors help Aesop Academy convert that existing creator reach into a public-good learning system.

Recruit

Subsidize

Sponsor dollars can underwrite seats, founding patron access, youth pathways, and worker cohorts.

Protect

Keep trust intact

Sponsor value

Recognition without control.

Sponsors should be visibly thanked for expanding access, but the integrity of the learning platform has to stay clean.

+Public sponsor recognition on mission and access pages.
+Impact reports showing seats funded, workshops supported, and communities reached.
+Optional named cohorts or workshop series, clearly labeled as sponsored.
+No influence over rankings, curriculum integrity, certification governance, or expert quality scoring.

The trust boundary is non-negotiable.

Sponsors fund access, reach, and mission delivery. They do not control expert selection, content rankings, AI quality scoring, patron recognition, forum access, certification governance, or what gets taught. That boundary is what lets Aesop Academy ask for support without compromising the credibility of 25experts.
