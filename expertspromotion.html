Creator advancement

Earn your place.
Build your community.

Advancement at 25experts is earned through two things: the quality of what you teach and the loyalty of the patrons who trust you. No follower counts. No paid placements. Just proof you're making people better at their work.

The four designations

Where you start. Where you can go.

Every creator enters as a Contributor. The path leads to Expert — and from there, to one of two co-equal top designations: Fellow (earned through promotion) or Founding Expert (reserved for vertical founders).

Path one

Contributor → Expert

To advance from Contributor to Expert, you need to prove two things consistently: that your content makes people better at their work, and that patrons trust you enough to pay for more of it.

100
Minimum patrons
The floor. You need at least 100 paying patrons before an Expert review can begin.
60%
Patron retention
More than half your patrons renew month-over-month. Retention proves real value, not just a good first impression.
3 mo
Sustained consistency
Three consecutive months of qualifying content across at least two formats — video, blog, workshop, or forum.
🎓

Content quality

Reviewed by the editorial team against the platform's quality standard: practical, accurate, original, and genuinely useful for working professionals.

  • At least 6 qualifying original videos
  • At least 3 published blog posts
  • At least 1 live workshop delivered on the platform
  • Completion rates above platform average
  • Content saved or shared by patrons
💬

Community contribution

Experts are teachers — and the best teachers show up in the room. Forum presence and learner support are part of the Expert standard.

  • At least 10 forum replies marked helpful or accepted
  • At least 2 expert-highlighted answers in patron rooms
  • No active moderation flags or trust violations
  • Patron questions answered within 5 business days on average
📈

Learner outcomes

The platform tracks real learning signals — not just views.

  • Workshop challenge completion rate ≥ 25%
  • Learning path completions attributed to your content
  • Patron testimonials or outcome reports (optional but weighted)
  • Content cited in forum answers by other members
🔄

Patron loyalty

Patrons vote every month when they renew. Retention is the most honest signal of whether your content is worth paying for.

  • 60%+ patron renewal rate over trailing 3 months
  • No pattern of refund requests or disputes
  • Patron growth trend (flat or rising)
  • Live event attendance above 30% of patrons
Expert review gate — all of the following must be true
✓Minimum 100 active patrons
✓Patron renewal rate ≥ 60% over 3 consecutive months
✓At least 6 qualifying original videos, 3 blog posts, and 1 live workshop
✓Editorial quality review passed (initiated by platform, not creator)
✓At least 10 accepted forum contributions
✓No active moderation flags
✓3 months of sustained, consistent content across at least 2 formats

"Expert status is not a reward for showing up. It is recognition that your patrons trust you and your content is making them better. Both have to be true."

— 25experts Standards Board
Path two

Expert → Fellow

Fellowship is the highest designation an Expert can earn through promotion. It requires 250 active patrons, sustained retention, and a Standards Board review. Unlike Founding Expert — which belongs to vertical founders — Fellowship is the path every Expert can work toward.

250
Minimum patrons
The floor for Fellow consideration. 250 active paying patrons demonstrates real, sustained demand for your teaching.
75%
Patron retention
Three quarters of your patrons renew month-over-month over at least 6 consecutive months.
12 mo
Time as Expert
At least 12 months as a verified Expert in good standing before a Fellow review can begin.
⭐

Platform influence

Fellows define the quality bar for their entire vertical, not just their own content.

  • Content consistently ranked at the top of its vertical by completion and saves
  • Other creators cite or build on your work in the forum
  • Patron testimonials demonstrate measurable career or skill impact
  • Learners complete entire learning paths anchored by your content
🏛️

Governance readiness

Fellows hold a seat on the Standards Board. The role carries real responsibility.

  • At least 12 months as a verified Expert in good standing
  • Demonstrated commitment to the Aesop Academy mission and access values
  • No unresolved disputes, flags, or conduct issues
  • Willingness to participate in quarterly Standards Board reviews
📡

Vertical authority

Fellows are recognized as authoritative voices in their domain — on and off the platform.

  • Actively publishing in at least 3 content formats
  • Live workshop attendance consistently above 40% of patrons
  • External credibility (industry recognition, published work, demonstrated expertise)
  • Patron base growing or stable over 6+ months
🤝

Community trust

Fellows set the tone for the entire platform community.

  • Patron renewal rate ≥ 75% over 6 consecutive months
  • Active and respected presence in the forum and patron rooms
  • Patron feedback consistently positive across content quality, availability, and responsiveness
  • No history of patron disputes or content integrity questions
Fellow review gate — Standards Board decision
✓Minimum 250 active patrons
✓Patron renewal rate ≥ 75% over 6 consecutive months
✓At least 12 months as a verified Expert in good standing
✓Content ranked at the top of the vertical for quality, completion, and saves
✓Demonstrated vertical authority — on platform and externally
✓Standards Board review and majority vote
⭐

What about Founding Expert?

Founding Expert is a parallel designation — not a promotion path. The 5 founders of each vertical hold Founding Expert status permanently. It carries the same privileges, revenue share, and Standards Board seat as Fellow. The difference is origin: Founding Experts built the vertical. Fellows earned their way to the top after it was built.

"Fellowship is the platform saying: you earned this. Your patrons stayed, your content held up, and your community trusts you. That is the standard — and it is available to every Expert who meets it."

— 25experts Standards Board
What we don't measure

Advancement is never about these.

These signals are explicitly excluded from advancement decisions to protect the integrity of the platform.

Follower count

How big your audience is on other platforms has no bearing on your tier. What matters is what your patrons here think of your work.

Social media presence

You don't need a large following to be an Expert. You need patrons who renew, learners who improve, and content that holds up to review.

Paid promotion

There is no path to advancement through sponsorships, advertising, or financial relationships with the platform. Period.

Seniority alone

Being here a long time doesn't earn a promotion. Only continued proof of quality, patron loyalty, and community contribution does.
