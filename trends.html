Why this page exists

Trend awareness is part of AI literacy.

Aesop Academy's mission is to close the AI literacy gap before it becomes a permanent opportunity gap. Understanding what is actually moving in AI — and why — is foundational to that mission. You can't use AI well if you don't know what's changing and what still matters.

Signal over noise

The T/V/N/C scoring framework teaches learners to evaluate AI developments critically — not just react to whatever headline is loudest today.

Community direction

Patron voting means learners shape what experts cover next. The platform follows the community's real questions, not a content algorithm.

Write-ins give agency

Anyone can surface a topic that matters to their actual work. Trends is not a passive feed — patrons are co-editors of what gets explored.

Fresh curriculum

Vote results feed directly into what experts prepare for workshops and blogs the following week, keeping the curriculum responsive to where AI is actually going.

25experts is the community platform of Aesop Academy, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. No advertising. No algorithmic ranking. Expert content is evaluated on quality, and trends are scored on actual signal — not engagement metrics.
