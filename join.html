Questions

Good questions.

What happens when my 7-day trial ends?

If you don't cancel before the trial ends, your subscription starts at the standard rate for the experts you chose. You'll get a reminder before it converts.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Cancel any time and your patron access continues through the end of the current billing month. No partial refunds, no commitments.

What does "single-use access code" mean for live events?

Each live workshop generates a unique code for your account that admits one person. Codes can't be shared — this keeps live events genuinely exclusive to patrons and protects the experts' direct access model.

If I follow one expert, do I get blog posts from all experts?

Yes. Full blog access is a platform-wide patron benefit. Patronize any one expert and you can read every blog post from every expert on the platform.

How do I know if a Founding Patron spot is still available?

Each expert's profile shows how many of their 25 founding spots remain. When the last spot is claimed, the founding rate closes permanently for that expert.

What if an expert I patronize leaves the platform?

If an expert leaves, your subscription to them ends and you won't be charged for the following month. Your access to platform-wide benefits (blogs, 30+ day replays) continues through any other experts you patronize.

Is 25experts really a nonprofit?

The AI vertical of 25experts operates as a program of Aesop Academy, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. No algorithm is optimizing against your learning. The mission is AI literacy — full stop.
