Access model

Free discovery. $14/month per Expert for deeper access.

Aesop Academy's mission means access cannot be an afterthought. The platform must help people learn even when they cannot pay full price, while making paid patronage clear: you pay for each Expert you choose to follow deeply.

Free learner access

Built for discovery, trust, and public benefit across 25experts.

  • Creator profiles and topic pages
  • Short-form clips and selected lessons
  • Public ranking pages
  • Weekly digest
  • Limited social search
  • Public live event calendar
  • Scholarship or sponsored-access pathways

$14/month per Expert

Built for depth, direct access, progress, and identity with the specific Experts you choose.

  • Full content from that Expert
  • Live Q&A participation with that Expert
  • Expert-specific live events, replays, and workshops
  • AI chatbot companion for that Expert's content and learning path
  • Community access around that Expert's work
  • Implementation challenges tied to that Expert's lessons
  • Progress tracking for the Experts and tracks you support

Example: following one Expert deeply is $14/month and unlocks that Expert's paid content, live events, Q&A access, replays, community layer, and AI companion support. Following three Experts deeply is $42/month. Free platform discovery remains available, but live-event access and AI companion access are paid features.
