The trust boundary is non-negotiable.

All support — individual or corporate — funds access, reach, and mission delivery. It does not buy expert rankings, patron recognition, forum access, content placement, certification governance, or what gets taught. That boundary is what lets Aesop Academy ask for support without compromising the credibility of 25experts.
