The weekly experience

Not a library you open once and forget.

25experts runs on a live weekly rhythm — so there's always something happening, always a reason to show up, and always a concrete next step. Spaced practice with active recall is how humans actually build skill. This structure is designed around that, not around content volume.

Monday

New workflow

A practical AI lesson or workflow you can apply during the week — ready when you start your morning.

Wednesday

Live workshop

Expert-led Q&A, office hours, or deep-dive session. Ask your actual question. There's no algorithm deciding who gets heard.

Friday

Your challenge

A small implementation task that turns watching into doing. Build a real workflow, prompt, or automation you can use next week.

Example: Build a client onboarding workflow that saves 3 hours/week — submit it and get feedback from an expert.

Weekly

AI companion

Catch up on missed workshops, ask questions about anything you watched, and get your next best action — personalized to your learning path and the experts you follow.

Questions

Things you're probably wondering.

Is this just another content platform?

No. The core product is direct access to the experts — live Q&A, office hours, and AMAs. The content exists to support your learning, not to maximize your watch time. The weekly rhythm, professional rooms, and catch-up feed are designed around what actually helps people make progress.

Why is this a non-profit?

Because AI literacy is a public good. The people who understand AI will shape what comes next. The ones who don't will be shaped by it. Aesop Academy's mission is to close that gap — for youth, for working professionals, globally. A non-profit structure means that mission cannot be overridden by a business model.

How is this different from just following experts on YouTube?

YouTube gives you content but limits the relationship. Here you can ask questions live, join a professional room with people who have your exact challenges, find content by what you're trying to solve rather than what the algorithm surfaces, and earn credentials based on what you've learned.

What if I'm a student or can't afford the paid tier?

The free tier is real and permanent — not a trial. If you're a student or youth learner, free access pathways are available through Aesop Academy's mission funding. Email info@aesopacademy.org.

Is this a startup that might disappear?

No. 25experts.com is a program of Aesop Academy, a 501(c)(3) non-profit. It is grant and donor funded. It does not have a runway. It has a mission.
