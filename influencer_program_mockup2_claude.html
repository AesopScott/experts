Questions

The honest answers

Things you're probably wondering.

Do I have to leave YouTube, Substack, or Patreon?

No. Zero exclusivity required. Experts is an additional income stream alongside your existing channels. Keep everything you have. Add this on top.

Who owns my content?

You do, always. You grant us a non-exclusive license to host and stream your content. You can remove it anytime with 30 days notice. Your content, your rights.

What if my patrons don't migrate?

We provide a full migration kit - personalized page, pre-written patron email, and a permanently locked founding rate below standard price - so patrons have a clear reason and a simple path to move.

How does the Creator Growth Fund work?

10% of monthly net platform revenue goes into the fund. Your share equals your proportion of total platform patrons. Calculated automatically, shown in your dashboard in real time, paid monthly alongside your subscription revenue split.

What's the founding creator equity offer?

The top 5–10 founding creators are offered 0.1–0.5% equity in the platform, vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. For creators who commit early and help shape the product. Details discussed on the application call.

What content topics do you accept?

Practical AI education: prompt engineering, agentic workflows, AI for business, ML fundamentals, AI ethics and governance, generative design. If your audience is professionals learning to use AI at work, you're a fit.
