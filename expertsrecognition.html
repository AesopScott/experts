Expert recognition program

Recognition for experts should reward value, not volume.

Experts earn standing primarily through quality evaluations of their content, and secondarily through the number of patrons subscribed to them. The reward is increased marketing exposure that helps potential patrons discover the experts doing the strongest work.

Expert standing becomes visible Recognition can affect expert profile emphasis, expert leaderboards, verified badges, forum authority, featured placement, and how much platform marketing attention an expert receives.
P

Content Quality

Content quality is the primary expert recognition signal. It measures whether the expert's videos, blogs, and workshops are useful, clear, current, and valued by patrons.

What it leads to

Expert leaderboard placement, profile emphasis, quality badges, and increased marketing exposure to potential patrons.

How points are earned

  • +AI quality evaluations of videos, blogs, and workshops
  • +Human review for top-ranked or disputed content
  • +Patron reviews that show usefulness, clarity, and practical value
  • +Freshness and accuracy checks for fast-moving AI topics

Visible recognition

  • ✓Content-quality leaderboard ranking
  • ✓Profile badges such as Top Rated Expert
  • ✓Featured placement on expert discovery pages
  • ✓Increased marketing exposure to potential patrons
Q

Subscribed Patrons

Subscribed patrons are the second major recognition signal. This measures whether patrons choose to follow and pay for that expert over time.

What it leads to

Patron-growth badges, stronger expert profile emphasis, ranking advantages, and marketing allocation signals.

How points are earned

  • +Paid patrons subscribed to the expert
  • +Free patrons converting to paid for that expert
  • +Patron retention over time
  • +Healthy engagement among that expert's patrons

Visible recognition

  • ✓Subscribed-patron leaderboard ranking
  • ✓Profile badges such as Patron Favorite
  • ✓Input into marketing-dollar allocation
  • ✓Evidence for Contributor to Specialist advancement
L

Live Engagement

Live engagement measures whether patrons attend workshops and return for the expert's live access.

What it leads to

Workshop badges, live-event prominence, stronger expert profile emphasis, scheduling priority, and additional promotion.

How points are earned

  • +Workshop attendance by paid patrons
  • +Repeat attendance over multiple workshops
  • +Useful Q&A and patron reviews after the event
  • +Workshop clips or reminders shared by patrons

Visible recognition

  • ✓Live engagement leaderboard ranking
  • ✓Workshop authority badges
  • ✓Priority placement in workshop calendar promotion
  • ✓Signal for expert reliability and patron access
C

Community Contribution

Community contribution measures how well the expert supports forums, patron questions, and platform trust.

What it leads to

Forum authority badges, expert profile emphasis, Super Patron forum authority, and stronger marketing recognition.

How points are earned

  • +Answering useful forum questions
  • +Highlighting strong patron reviews or posts
  • +Creating workshop follow-ups from forum questions
  • +Helping maintain high-signal discussion spaces

Visible recognition

  • ✓Community contribution leaderboard ranking
  • ✓Forum authority badges on expert replies
  • ✓Higher visibility inside Super Patron forums
  • ✓Evidence for platform trust and expert status
G

Community Growth

Community growth measures how much the expert helps 25experts reach new audiences.

What it leads to

Growth badges, expert leaderboard standing, featured campaigns, and stronger public discovery among potential patrons.

How points are earned

  • +Sharing 25experts content with outside audiences
  • +Bringing patrons from existing channels
  • +Creating social posts that drive profile visits or follows
  • +Participating in platform campaigns or launch pushes

Visible recognition

  • ✓Community-growth leaderboard placement
  • ✓Growth badges on expert profile
  • ✓Campaign and discovery placement
  • ✓Signal for marketing-dollar allocation
Not output volume

Production alone is not the score.

Videos, blogs, and workshops matter because they can be evaluated for quality and value. Raw quantity should not outrank strong evaluations or real subscribed patron demand.

Public vs internal

Show recognition, keep scoring auditable.

Public pages can show badges, tiers, and rankings. Internal systems should keep the detailed scoring model, anti-gaming checks, and dispute review trail.

Patron-connected

Recognition should create more discovery.

The practical reward for strong expert contribution is more platform marketing: better discovery placement, featured campaigns, and more exposure to potential patrons.

Expert recognition feeds the Expert program.

Recognition should help identify who deserves more visibility, who qualifies for stronger expert status, and which experts are creating the highest-quality content. Patron count matters, but the system should avoid becoming a raw popularity contest by weighting quality evaluations first.

  • Founding Expert / Expert Fellow: verified authority and launch commitment
  • Specialist: demonstrated quality, audience, and patron value
  • Contributor: can compete upward through quality and engagement
  • All tiers: subject to anti-gaming and human review
