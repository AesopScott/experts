How 25experts works

A focused network of trusted experts.

The model is five focused verticals, each anchored by five verified Founding Experts — 25 experts when complete. The first vertical is AI and Automation.

Not another feed. A learning system.

25experts combines what scattered platforms cannot: expert curation, live learning, practical workflows, role-based communities, AI companion support, and a clear path from discovery to mastery.

  • Free discovery and mission-funded access pathways
  • Paid patronage for the individual Experts learners choose
  • Live events, Q&A, and applied workshops
  • Quality-ranked content and transparent standards
