Questions

The honest answers

Things you're probably wondering.

Do I have to leave YouTube, Substack, or Patreon?

No. Zero exclusivity required. Experts is an additional income stream alongside your existing channels. Keep everything you have. Add this on top.

Who owns my content?

You do. Always. You grant us a non-exclusive license to host and stream your content. You can remove it anytime with 30 days notice. Your content, your rights.

What if my patrons don't migrate?

The 90-Day Earnings Guarantee covers exactly this scenario. If you earn less than your current Patreon income in any of your first three months, we make up the difference. Migration is also supported with a white-glove kit — your patrons get a personalized invitation and a permanently lower founding rate.

How does the marketing pool work?

10% of monthly net platform revenue goes into a Creator Growth Fund. Your share equals your proportion of total platform patrons. It's calculated automatically, shown in your dashboard in real time, and paid monthly alongside your subscription revenue.

What's the founding creator equity offer?

The top 5–10 founding creators are offered 0.1–0.5% equity in the platform, vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. This is for creators who commit early and help shape the product. Details discussed on application call.

What topics do you accept?

Practical AI education. Prompt engineering, agentic workflows, AI for business, machine learning fundamentals, AI ethics and governance, generative design. If your audience is professionals learning to use AI at work, you're a fit.
