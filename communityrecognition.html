Community Recognition system

Four ways patrons earn standing.

The recognition system is simple: patrons earn recognition by consuming expert content, contributing useful reviews and forum posts, showing up consistently, and helping expert content reach new audiences.

Recognition becomes visible Points influence leaderboards. Badges appear on patron profiles and forum posts. Strong engagement can unlock higher-tier forums, including Super Patron spaces.
C

Capability

Capability measures how deeply a patron engages with expert content across the platform.

What it leads to

Points, leaderboard placement, profile badges, forum-post badges, and higher-tier forum access.

How points are earned

  • +Watching expert videos
  • +Attending live workshops
  • +Reading expert blog posts
  • +Following multiple experts inside a domain

Visible recognition

  • ✓Capability leaderboard ranking
  • ✓Profile badges such as Video Watcher, Blog Reader, and Workshop Attendee
  • ✓Badge color and emphasis on forum posts
  • ✓Unlock path toward Super Patron forums
R

Contribution

Contribution measures how much useful context a patron adds back into the platform.

What it leads to

Contribution badges, visible recognition, stronger forum identity, and higher-tier forum access.

How points are earned

  • +Posting video reviews
  • +Posting blog reviews
  • +Posting workshop reviews
  • +Making useful forum posts and replies

Visible recognition

  • ✓Reviewer badges on the patron profile
  • ✓Forum-post badges with different color and emphasis
  • ✓Recognition for useful reviews and discussion posts
  • ✓Unlock path toward Super Patron forums
S

Consistency

Consistency measures whether a patron builds a real rhythm week to week.

What it leads to

Profile badges, forum-post badges, and placement on a consistency leaderboard.

How points are earned

  • +Returning weekly to watch, read, or attend
  • +Contributing reviews or forum posts week to week
  • +Growing capability over a sustained period
  • +Maintaining a balanced engagement rhythm across content types

Visible recognition

  • ✓Consistency badges on the patron profile
  • ✓Streak or rhythm markers on forum posts
  • ✓Placement on the consistency leaderboard
  • ✓Recognition for sustained engagement, not one-time spikes
G

Community growth

Community growth measures how patrons help expert content reach people beyond the platform.

What it leads to

A dedicated community-growth leaderboard, profile badges, forum-post badges, and social sharing recognition.

How points are earned

  • +Sharing expert videos to other social platforms
  • +Sharing expert blog posts
  • +Sharing workshop pages, clips, or reminders
  • +Helping new patrons discover experts and domains

Visible recognition

  • ✓Community-growth leaderboard placement
  • ✓Profile badges for sharing and amplification
  • ✓Forum-post badges that show growth contribution
  • ✓Recognition for helping the platform and experts reach new audiences

Higher-tier forums come from capability and contribution.

Super Patron forums should unlock through a combination of content engagement and useful contribution. Patrons should not get higher-tier access just by being loud; they should earn it by watching, reading, attending, reviewing, and posting in ways that help the platform.

  • Capability unlock: content engagement threshold
  • Contribution unlock: review and forum-post threshold
  • Consistency signal: sustained week-to-week rhythm
  • Growth signal: meaningful social sharing
